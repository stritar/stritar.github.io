About Me

I am a Digital Designer with a broad range of skills and experiences, primarily focused on UI design.

I first started making websites more than ten years ago. Since then I learned how to write code, make 3D models, illustrate, organise and lead a team, consult start-ups or corporations. I established a particular interest in UI where I am able to fuse together all of the acquired skills. I am occasionally hired to assist in developing a business idea or a product. I work internationally and can adapt and relocate if necessary.

I spend most of my free time reading, flying long-haul or listening to podcasts while going on long walks - I believe that the only way to explore world is on foot. Some of my heroes are Christopher Hitchens, Nick Cave & Warren Ellis, Elon Musk and Dwight D. Eisenhower. I have become obsessed with skydiving and am in a process of getting licensed.
My motto is Nil Desperandum - Never despair.
