ABOUT ME

I choose not to label myself directly. Being creative, I'm leaning towards digital and industrial design, illustration and other creative projects.

I have more than five years of active experience in web design including creative direction, planning and wire-framing, as well as leading small development teams and offering consultation to other studios. I have sufficient knowledge of HTML and CSS to create a website from a scratch. I also combine my other skills, such as 3D modelling and animation, to push the creative edge further up the scale.

I am a podcast and online lecture addict. My alter-ego writes poetry and short stories. I love long walks especially on the streets of worlds largest cities. Cormac McCarthy and Christopher Hitchens are two of my favourite authors. Nick Cave and Warren Ellis is my favourite music duo. Johnnie Walker Black is my favourite blend.
