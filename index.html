ABOUT ME

I find it hard to label myself exactly. Being creative, I'm leaning towards UI and industrial design, architecture, illustration and other such crafts.

I have a more than four years of experience in UI design including creative direction, planning and wire-framing, consulting and having responsibility over small teams of developers on many different projects. I know enough code to build a website from scratch - like this one. I also have enough knowledge of 3D modelling that helps me to add a significant value to many projects, it also helps me to visualise my product and architecture ideas. I write poetry, can play some guitar and love long walks, listening to podcasts. I keep on learning and try to keep my life from the state of stagnation.
"Nil Desperandum" is my motto.
