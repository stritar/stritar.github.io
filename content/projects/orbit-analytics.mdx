> Users don't want a dashboard. They want an answer.

## The challenge

- Forty widgets on one screen, no hierarchy.
- Every team asked for "just one more chart."
- New users bounced within the first session.

## Approach

We reframed the surface around three recurring questions and let everything
else live a click deeper. The default view answers *what changed and why*
before it offers *everything you could possibly slice*.

- **+44%** week-one activation.
- **−30%** time to first insight.
- A widget budget that finally held.
