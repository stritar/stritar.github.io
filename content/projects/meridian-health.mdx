## Overview

Managing a chronic condition means juggling clinics, pharmacies and portals.

make the next right step obvious.

> Calm is a feature. For anxious users, it might be the feature.

## The challenge

- Information lived in five places and agreed on nothing.
- Reminders felt like nagging, not support.
- Trust was fragile and easily lost.

## Approach

We built a single timeline as the home of the app — past, present and next —
and let everything else hang off it. Tone and motion were tuned to reassure
rather than alarm.

## Outcomes

- **+51%** medication adherence among pilot users.
- **4.8** average store rating after launch.
- Support contacts about "where do I find…" dropped sharply.
