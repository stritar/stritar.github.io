About Me

I am a full-stack digital designer with a broad range of skills and experiences, focused on UI and product design.

I have been a digital designer for 12 years, during which time I learned how to write the code, 3D model, illustrate and develop products. My particular interest is in UI where I am able to practice the whole spectrum of skills. I am occasionally hired to assist in developing a business idea. I also consult start-ups and established firms. I work internationally and can adapt and relocate if necessary.

I spend most of my free time developing my personal projects, reading, flying long-haul while listening to podcasts, and exploring world on long walks. Some of my heroes are Christopher Hitchens, Nick Cave & Warren Ellis, Elon Musk and Dwight D. Eisenhower. I have become obsessed with skydiving and am in a process of getting licensed.
My motto is Nil Desperandum - Never despair.
